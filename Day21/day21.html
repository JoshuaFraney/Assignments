Things I like about this class
  • Our teacher knows his stuff.

  • The lectures prepare us well for the afternoon assignments.
  • I like how the breaks split up the day.
Things I think would improve the class
  • Letting us leave before 5 when the class is finished ahead of time.
  • A few more hands on examples of more difficult topics in the lecture before we get to the assignments.
  • The TIYO files could be shown sooner so we can be prepared for upcoming topics.
  • After lunch nap time ;).
